Our Story



Founded in 2010, ROYAL CART began as a small family workshop with a passion for creating beautiful, handcrafted furniture. What started as a humble operation has grown into a trusted name in home furnishings, serving customers across the country.

Our journey began when our founder, Sarah Johnson, recognized a need for high-quality, thoughtfully designed furniture that combines timeless aesthetics with modern functionality. She assembled a team of skilled artisans and designers who share her vision for creating pieces that stand the test of time.

Today, we continue to honor our roots by maintaining the highest standards of craftsmanship while embracing innovation in design and sustainability. Each piece in our collection tells a story of dedication, attention to detail, and love for the art of furniture making.
